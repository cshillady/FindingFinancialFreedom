Frequently Asked Questions

What is Finding Financial Freedom (FFF)?
Finding Financial Freedom is a management app that will help users achieve their goals at whatever pace they set.

How do I delete income, from my income page?
Go to your income page and click the red button that says delete.

Can I change the life expectancy on my retirement calculator?
The default is set to 85, however if you click and hold the ball and drag it, it will change up to 100 and as low as 70.

How do I change my Password?
last request, erase everything and type in the new password. Finally click submit and password will update, this process can be repeated for all other user information.

How does the app work?
Users will have the chance to create a profile where they input their expenses, income, savings and goals. All the data will then be used to calculate how much each user must put aside for each goal.

How would I find financial freedom?
FFF also provides a retirement calculator that gives the user the opportunity see when they will be ready to retire.

How do I add goals?
Go to your goals page. Click "add goal", enter the amount followed by the current amount, category, and date range. Finally hit submit.
